About the Course

One Diploma, Two Specialisations, Many Opportunities

This is for you if you are keen to be part of the new generation of entrepreneurs and business professionals in the digital age.

Be equipped with a diverse range of skills and make a difference in the business world. Pursue a dual track in fields such as International Business, Marketing & Digital Communications and E-Commerce & Retail Management.

You will learn fundamental business subjects and critical, specialised skills such as digital marketing, business analytics and design thinking.

This diploma lets you . . .

  • Learn the architecture of businesses and how to succeed as a professional or an entrepreneur.
  • Be enriched by a dual-specialisation curriculum and pick from 12 specialisation combinations.
  • Enhance your market value with your experience in working on industry projects in which you solve real business challenges.

Benefits

  • Acquire in-demand skills in emerging business areas such as Customer Experience & Relationship Management, Human Capital Management, International Business Management, E-Commerce & Retail Management, Supply Chain & Logistics Management, Event Design & Management and Marketing & Digital Communications.
  • Interdisciplinary training of business professionals, tapping the pooled resources and strengths of various business disciplines and industry partnerships.
  • Look forward to exciting career opportunities with top businesses.

NYP Professional Competency Model (NYP-PCM) - its unique features

This diploma will be taught using the NYP-PCM starting AY2024.

Industry Relevance

Endorsed by industry leaders.

Professional Accreditation

You gain additional industry certifications while you complete your diploma, putting you ahead of the pack.

Agile Response

The curriculum reacts dynamically and quickly to industry changes. You'll be equipped with the latest skills & knowledge.

Integrated Learning

Concurrently learn multiple disciplines.

The NYP-PCM for the Diploma in Business Management consists of:

23 Competency Units
Within each Competency Unit, the skills and knowledge of different disciplines are integrated and taught holistically. This enables learners to fulfil specific work tasks upon completion.
6 Course Competencies

Each Course Competency consists of a collection of Competency Units that develops specific learning outcomes and competencies.
2 Competency Canvases

Each Competency Canvas consists of a collection of Competency Units and a Work- Integration Unit (WIU), which is a workplace-contextualised project. Competency Canvases help learners develop and demonstrate their abilities to perform work tasks at a higher proficiency.
1 Teaching Enterprise Project

1 Internship Programme

11 General Studies Modules

These modules allow you to explore other areas of interest beyond your chosen area of specialisation.

Application of Business Fundamentals [60 hours]

In current workplace, employers are looking for graduates who have a holistic understanding of todayâ€™s complex and dynamic business environment and have the ability to connect the dots of business functions, relationships, systems and resources. Thus, having a good understanding of business fundamentals is essential for all business professionals. This CmU covers the ecosystem of business organisation and environment and provides the grounding and interlinks of the key business functions and skills of business - human resource management and administration, supply chain management, finance and accounting, marketing, international business, operations management, laws and policies.


Applied Economics for Business [60 hours]

In a dynamic business environment, individuals and business organisations need to apply microeconomic and macroeconomic analyses to evaluate the impacts of changing economic climate. In this CmU, learners will acquire the knowledge on how individuals and businesses make decisions about consumption and production based on market structures, and the role of government in influencing these decisions. Learners will also be equipped with analytical skills to assess the impact on businesses from changing economic factors such as a countryâ€™s national income, business cycle, price levels of goods and services, fiscal & monetary policies and international trade issues.


Applied Statistics for Business [60 hours]

In a fast-paced business environment, employers are looking for graduates who are able to analyse, understand and apply data to make optimal business decisions.  In this CmU, learners will be equipped with the knowledge and skills to transform raw data into useful information and apply the insights obtained to make informed business decisions. Specifically, learners will be taught descriptive statistics, regression and correlation analysis, discrete and continuous probability distributions, statistical estimation, hypothesis testing, and fundamental artificial intelligence (AI) concepts and tools to solve real-life business problems.


Business IT & Spreadsheet Modelling [60 hours]

The ability to analyse is an indispensable skill in the business world as it helps organisations make better data-driven decisions.  In this digital world, it is also equally important to be able to ensure effective data storing and reliable data flow by using the right technology. In this CmU, learners will gain essential knowledge and skills in analysing data using system software in presenting business solutions effectively. This CmU will also provide opportunities for learners to build and enhance their knowledge in key digital transformations. Specifically, learners will be equipped with knowledge in relevant data structures, databases and cloud computing.


Financial Accounting & Interpretation [60 hours]

Employers are always looking for graduates who have good understanding of accounting concepts and practices to properly account for business transactions and assess business financial performance. In this CmU, learners will learn to apply accounting concepts and principles for the recording of business transactions, and the preparation of year-end adjustments and financial statements. In addition, learners will acquire competencies in interpreting financial statements and information for various stakeholders.


International Business Analysis [60 hours]

Globalisation has led to increased international trade. Many small and medium enterprises are joining the leagues of multinational companies to buy and sell overseas. Thus, it is important for business owners and investors to assess the attractiveness of new foreign markets. This CmU will equip learners with the competency in the identification of desirable foreign markets using environmental scanning and basic country analysis. In addition, learners will be taught various market entry strategies for potential expansion.


Marketing [60 hours]

Good marketing is no accident, but a result of careful planning and creative execution. Companies are constantly looking for marketing professionals who can help them to analyse the business environment, build strong brands and achieve profitable customer relationships. In this CmU, learners will be equipped with the knowledge and skills to conduct market research, glean market insights and make the best possible tactical and strategic decisions for brands based on the companyâ€™s business objectives.


Professional Business Communication [60 hours]

Professional business communication that is driven in companies determine the success of their operations and business.  Strong communication skills are needed in AI-driven and sustainable workplaces.  In this CmU, learners will master must-have business communication skills to achieve professional success. This CmU builds learners' communication competencies in applying effective business writing strategies and persuasive presentation techniques to influence and win over co-workers and customers in person and online. Learners will also sharpen their communication proficiencies to enhance personal branding and workplace relationships that advance careers. Learners will also master the art of business storytelling to better connect with audiences, especially for sustainability.


Strategic Business Communication [45 hours]

Companies engage in strategic business communication.  Skilled strategic communications cultivate positive relationships, promote inclusive social well-being, and safeguard their organisations' reputations through effective interpersonal and digital communication.  A strategic focus includes communicating sustainability effectively tailored to different circumstances and cultural contexts.  In this CmU, learners will demonstrate defining strategic communication skills to thrive in increasingly diverse, multicultural and digital workplaces. Specifically, learners will apply strategic and critical thinking that are part of strategic communication skills to achieve bias-free communication, foster inclusiveness and negotiate challenging exchanges in person or virtual.


User-Centred Design [45 hours]

Companies need to thrive in a business environment often termed as VUCA - volatile, uncertain, complex and ambiguous - and to survive and thrive in this world of disruption, the name of the game is "innovation". Companies value employees who can innovate and develop solutions that meet the needs of its users. This CmU will provide opportunities for learners to practise and experience the key stages of Design Thinking, such as being empathetic to users, identifying pain-points, generating creative ideas, prototyping and testing the innovations, as learners apply the various tools and methods through collaborative learning.

Data Science Fundamentals and Analytics [60 hours]

As businesses move toward a digitalised future, data will be gathered in ever greater quantity and variety. The skill to understand and make sense of data therefore becomes ever more important. In this CmU, learners will gain an awareness of the concepts of data science, with a specific focus on business analytics. Learners will be able to analyse data using exploratory data analytics through the application of data visualisation and will also be able to create a basic machine learning model for predictive analytics. These skills to organise and prepare data and apply data analysis techniques will allow learners to gain insights from data, aid business functions and support business decision making.


Financial Management & Analysis [60 hours]

Executives equipped with financial management skills add value to the management of business resources and processes. This CmU guides learners to analyse business performance through financial statements and appreciate the basics of working capital. Learners will learn to deploy time value of money concept and risk vis-Ã -vis returns to long term investment and financing decisions. Learners will also learn to evaluate the performance of operation using flexible budget and analyse relevant costs for short-term decision making.


People & Team Management [45 hours]

In a volatile and dynamic business environment, the ability to manage people and teams in an organisation that is made up of individuals of different ages, profiles and personalities, remains one of the most important skills to possess. It is also becoming increasingly challenging to manage the behaviour of professionals and create teams that are more satisfied, motivated, and engaged with organisational goals. Through this CmU, learners will be introduced to various managerial and leadership approaches to motivate and engage a team to collaborate and navigate through changes and the group development stages. They will also attain the competencies required to become an effective and efficient manager when carrying out managerial functions to optimise business performance.


Stakeholder Relationship Management [45 hours]

In today's highly competitive marketplace, complex issues in the business environment have started demanding the involvement of the stakeholders of an organisation. The identification of key stakeholders and their interests, and maintaining a strong relationship with them, has become imperative to optimise business performance. The value of a business is determined by how these key stakeholders interact and interface with one another. As the survival of any businesses is dependent on customers, the ability to strengthen and manage customers effectively has become pivotal in helping businesses to succeed in this competitive business industry. In this CmU, learners will be equipped with the knowledge of creating a stakeholder management plan that enhance the communication and engagement between them. Learners will also acquire competencies in customer relationship management strategies that create and deliver value for targeted customers in order to build mutually beneficial customer relationships.

  • Customer Experience Development Project (WIU)
  • Customer Management & Analytics
  • Experience Design & Management

Customer Experience Development Project (WIU) [45 hours]

In todayâ€™s fast-paced business environment, employers are looking for graduates who are able to plan and implement CRM strategies effectively.  In this WIU, learners will synthesise their knowledge in the analysis and strategy development for a business case. Specifically, learners will demonstrate competencies in areas such as Service Management, Experience Design & Management and User-Centred Design, among others. They will utilise skills in business IT and spreadsheet applications, written communication, and oral presentation.


Customer Management & Analytics [60 hours]

In today's highly complex and dynamic business environment, characterised by advanced technology and enhanced customer awareness, the ability to create and maintain strong customer relationships has become the cornerstone to a company's success and profitability. Through the adoption of CRM analytics tools, customer intelligence provides personalized insights into customers' behaviour that drives the business to new level of success. In this CmU, learners will be equipped with the skills and proficiency in the customer-centricity strategies to create a mutually sustainable profitable customer relationship.  Learners will acquire the competencies in using customer data and customer insight to increase customer value and customer equity. 


Experience Design & Management [60 hours]

In todayâ€™s evolving business landscape, being able to design and manage effective customer experiences that meet customer needs are highly valued by organisations. In this CmU, learners will be equipped with the knowledge and skills to create the right organisational culture, mindsets and processes that will transform customer experience across different types of interactions. Specifically, learners will use design thinking in conducting customer journey mapping to increase customer loyalty and advocacy. Upon completion of this CmU, learners can look forward to leveraging these acquired competencies in enhancing customer experience for companies in diverse industries.


Service Management [60 hours]

In todayâ€™s workplace, organisations need to employ graduates who not only possess customer-centric mindsets but are also equipped with the ability to plan and implement sound service management strategies. In this CmU, there will be opportunities for learners to understand how to turn business policy and objectives into reality. Learners will be able to apply key learnings in service concepts, service design delivery, service relationship quality and service excellence.


E-commerce & Retail Management Specialisation

  • Omnichannel Management & Analytics
  • Retail Buying & Category Management
  • Retail Management & Visual Merchandising
  • Retail Strategy & Sustainability Project (WIU)

Omnichannel Management & Analytics [60 hours]

In a fast-paced business environment, omni-channel retail businesses create a consistent on-brand experience from start to end for customers. It is vital to be able to analyse, decipher and apply data to make optimal business decisions. This CmU equips learners with Omni-channel management concepts and analytical capability for omni-channel retailing with a strategic approach by applying and integrating the essential business concepts of retail store operations and management. Learners will assess the effectiveness of omni-channel management of retailers by applying the technical concepts learnt in supply chain integration, social media marketing and best omni-channel retail standards to leverage on the opportunities present in the dynamic business landscape.


Retail Buying & Category Management [60 hours]

In a highly competitive retail landscape, successful retail businesses usually practise effective and efficient merchandising techniques and category management. In this CmU, learners will learn the concept of sourcing and retail merchandising through the application of buying techniques and category management. Learners will apply both qualitative and quantitative tools to plan and optimise buying plans that cater to the ever-changing demands of consumers and rapidly transforming retail space. Learners will be able to apply buying concepts learnt by developing plans and buying for lifestyle, luxury, fashion, beauty, home and grocery retail brands.


Retail Management & Visual Merchandising [60 hours]

The retail industry is filled with rapid changes and disruptions to store operations and management due to emerging technologies and changing customer expectations. This CmU equips learners with the required skills and techniques to better facilitate the physical and online stores through the application of retail management and visual merchandising concepts. Learners will be able to articulate the importance of store visual merchandising by applying various techniques in merchandise display, presentation methods and retail space planning to enhance retailersâ€™ branding and profitability. Learners will also have the opportunity to use application tools for effective store and merchandise layout presentation in retailing.


Retail Strategy & Sustainability Project (WIU) [45 hours]

In a highly competitive and challenging retail landscape, businesses are required to align business strategy and create innovative retail solutions to meet sustainability and profitability targets. In this WIU, the learners will learn to develop retail strategy by applying eCommerce & retail management concepts and optimising sales through integrated online and offline channels and digital marketing options. Learners will apply various merchandising policies, concepts in sustainable sourcing and ethics and visual display skills through developing and executing a merchandise plan in a web store. Through market research, data collection and analytics, learners will optimise the sales plan by refining their merchandise and social marketing plan.

  • HR Planning & Practices Project (WIU)
  • Performance & Rewards Management
  • Talent Attraction & Employee Relations
  • Talent Development & Management

HR Planning & Practices Project (WIU) [45 hours]

In todayâ€™s fast-paced business environment, employers are looking for graduates who are able to plan and implement HR strategies effectively.  In this WIU, learners will synthesise their knowledge in the analysis and strategy development for a business case. They will utilise skills in business IT and spreadsheet applications, written and strategic communication.


Performance & Rewards Management [60 hours]

In order to attract and retain talent in an ever-competitive workforce, it is important for organisations to provide a strong Performance and Rewards culture and practices. This CmU will allow learners to gain an understanding of compensation concepts (pay model, internal alignment, external competitiveness, employee contribution, benefits, and management of system) and to apply the theories in a real organisational context through practical assignments and projects.


Talent Attraction & Employee Relations [60 hours]

People are the largest investment any organisation will make into its future. Globally, attracting, engaging, and ultimately retaining talent will continue to drive a strong competitive advantage for organisations. Through this CmU, learners will develop competencies in sourcing and selecting candidates, and integrate them to an organisation through onboarding. Learners will also learn to build a reliable talent pipeline by developing a strong employer brand and conducting employee engagement initiatives to reinforce employees' commitment and connectivity to the organisation. They will be able to implement labour relations solutions to support, enhance and strengthen stakeholder relationships amongst trade unions, work councils and employee forums.


Talent Development & Management [60 hours]

In a volatile and dynamic business environment, the role of talent development and management has shifted. This CmU will provide learners with the competencies on learning needs analysis, facilitation skills, and training evaluation. Through this CmU, learners will be competent to equip talents with the right capabilities and prepare them well to contributing towards organisation's success.

  • International Freight Management
  • Supply Chain Solutioning Project (WIU)
  • Supply Chain Technologies & Operations

International Freight Management [60 hours]

A well-defined transportation system is a vital component of global supply chains which helps to promote major trade flows among economies. Having an effective and efficient transport system is critical in ensuring the uninterrupted movement of the physical trades. In this CmU, learners will be equipped with the skills in managing transportation systems and their processes of a business. They will develop competencies in performing day to day transportation of freight such as assessing the types of transport modes and carriers. Learners will be able to apply the shipper and carrier strategies to enhance a firmâ€™s competitive positioning to achieve cost savings, transport sustainability, enhance operating efficiencies, and streamline complex transport processes including customs clearance. They will also develop competencies in working with IT technologies and transport solutions to create or generate transport documents (including bills of lading and sea waybills) and reports for analysis.


Supply Chain Solutioning Project (WIU) [45 hours]

In a fast-paced business environment, employers are looking for graduates who are able to plan and provide Supply Chain Management solutioning effectively to their business challenges. In this CmU, learners will synthesise their knowledge in the analysis and solutioning for a business case. Specifically, learners will demonstrate competencies in areas such as Supply Chain Technologies and Operations, Warehouse Management and Analytics among others.


Supply Chain Technologies & Operations [60 hours]

Effective supply chain management impacts all areas and processes in an organisation and can significantly improve a firmâ€™s competitive position. In this CmU, the learners will acquire the concepts of supply chain management, technologies, and managerial practices in relation to the design and implementation of supply chain systems. Learners will also be equipped with analytical skills to assess the supply chain of a business with reference to the flow of products and information between the point of supply and the point of consumption in a global environment.


Warehouse Management & Analytics [60 hours]

Warehouse is an integral part to any supply chain of a business. The efficient and effective operation of a warehouse and management of its inventory have a large impact on the overall supply chain in a firm. In this CmU, the learners will apply the concepts of warehouse operations and inventory management in a supply chain. They will be able to formulate strategies to maximise efficiency in a warehouse. The learners will integrate technologies like barcoding and RFID and other automated inventory mechanisms to improve performances in basic warehouse processes of receiving, putaway, pick and pack and distribution. Learners will also be equipped with data analytic skills to perform analysis on demand forecast and optimise inventory management.


International Business Management Specialisation

  • Global Citizenry & Cultural Intelligence
  • International Operations Management

Global Citizenry & Cultural Intelligence [45 hours]

Business success requires more than just theories and knowledge. As companies internationalise, it is essential to understand the differences in culture and respect social norms of host countries. This CmU will equip learners with the nuances of cross-cultural interaction, heighten sensitivity to factors that may lead to cross-cultural misunderstanding, and strategise for reducing cross-cultural conflict in a changing global business environment. Learners will learn to manage communication and interactions across cultures, negotiate across cultures, understand cross cultural ethics and ESG (environmental, social and governance) issues.


International Operations Management [60 hours]

All goods move from factories to consumers and before that, materials move from their natural habitats to the factories.  Materials and goods do not stop moving and increasingly they move internationally.  Companies worldwide are involved in these movements directly or through the provision of financial and technology services to support them. Through this CmU, learners will learn to link international operations processes to provide critical advantages when they participate or manage business operations in any industry. Learners will gain deeper appreciation of the knowledge used to address business needs in terms of digitization and sustainability, that are key developments that shapes international operations.  Learners will be able to apply internationally accepted processes and systems to ensure smooth and efficient international operations.  Learners will also be equipped to analyse network of facilities and country infrastructure necessary to accomplish goals for international businesses.

  • Essentials of Event Management
  • Event Design & Production

Essentials of Event Management [45 hours]

In a competitive business environment, the ability to organise a well-executed event to elevate a companyâ€™s brand and reputation is a very much sought-after competency. Companies are on a constant lookout for candidates who can multitask and coordinate with various stakeholders in an event. This CmU equips learners the know-hows of end-to-end event planning and management, attaining the competencies in supply chain management, manpower and time management, location management, risk management and project management, all of which are crucial to be an outstanding event manager.


Event Design & Production [60 hours]

Innovation & Entrepreneurship Project (WIU) [45 hours]

This WIU culminates the competencies learnt in Year 2 and 3. Learners are required to apply the knowledge and skills gathered previously to test the viability of a business in preparation to launch it.  Learners will adopt the Agile Scrum framework to develop the skills to validate business model using Design Thinking and Lean Business Model Canvas methodologies, execute prototype testing to determine Problem-Solution Fit and Minimum Viable Product (MVP) testing to determine Product-Market Fit. They will also develop entrepreneurial attributes such as perseverance, resilience and risk-taking. This WIU will also provide opportunities for coaching from experienced business innovators currently from the industry, funding opportunities for pre-incubation and adoption of the business plan by industry partners.


Innovation & Viable Business Development [45 hours]

Studies have shown that although products and service innovation are essential for the initial success of a business, business model innovation can deliver more lasting competitive advantage, particularly in this disruptive era. This CmU aims to develop learnersâ€™ capability in business model innovation by applying Lean Business Model Canvas (LBMC) methodology on a business case. By analysing the evolving business models in terms of the integrated components of LBMC, learners develop in-depth knowledge and skills in enhancing business competitive advantage and value creation.
