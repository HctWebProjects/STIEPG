DIPLOMA IN COMPUTER ENGINEERING (S53, DCPE)

Creating Intelligent Systems for Modern Cities

Enter the thrilling world of computers, where intelligent systems shape our future. As we propel towards a future where autonomous vehicles, drones, and intelligent city management systems play a vital role, it's crucial to stay ahead in areas like Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT), data analytics, 5G, networking, and cybersecurity. At the Diploma in Computer Engineering (DCPE), you’ll harness these cutting-edge capabilities, empowering you to shape the future and create innovative solutions for a "Smart Nation" like Singapore.

If you're ready to embark on an adventure where technology meets innovation, the Diploma in Computer Engineering at SP is your gateway. Join us and gain the skills to thrive in the digital era!

5G & Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT)

Uncover the secrets of computer and electronic hardware design, software programming, networking, cyber security, and cloud computing. Delve into the exciting world of AIoT, where you'll work on projects that address real-world challenges, such as autonomous vehicles, vertical farming, and recycling solutions. Plus, with EEE exploring the integration of 5G connectivity into AIoT applications, you'll have a chance to be part of groundbreaking research.

Most comprehensive diploma-level computing course in Singapore

In a rapidly changing world, DCPE offers a comprehensive and flexible curriculum so that you can keep your career and study options wide open. Whether you're keen on Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Systems, Information Security, Electrical & Electronic Engineering, Data Science/AI, or beyond, DCPE sets you up to seize the opportunities that lie ahead.

Course Highlights

  • The most comprehensive curriculum of its kind, covering Embedded Systems, Software, 5G Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, Cloud Computing, Networking and Cyber Security.

  • Our curriculum is aligned with industrial certifications such as CCNA, Cisco-certified CyberOps Associate, CompTIA Security+, AWS-certified Cloud Practitioner, AWS-certified Solutions Architect Associate, and Huawei HCIA-AI to enhance your career prospects.
  • Our students have won prestigious scholarships and awards from CSIT, Singtel, A*STAR, DSTA, DSO, and Singapore Polytechnic.

What You Can Expect

  • Pick from a wide variety of specialisation options in Computer Applications, Cyber Security, Smart City Technologies, and Cloud Systems.
  • Pursue your passion through electives that can lead to a certificate or minor.
Read moreRead less
  • Gain exposure through a 6-week Overseas Immersion Programme to Japan.
  • Immerse in a 22-week internship with opportunities at reputable companies.
  • Take up an opportunity for a 12-month internship attachment to GovTech for exposure to Smart Nation projects.
  • Join the premier Engineering Academy programme and participate in local and overseas competitions.
  • Develop holistically through our CDIO (Conceive-Design-Implement-Operate) framework, used in top universities in the United States, Europe, and Australia.
  • Experience realistic learning using on-campus facilities such as Smart Connected Solutions Lab.
  • Join the SP-NUS Accelerated Pathway Programme (SP-NUS APP) or SP-SUTD Accelerated Pathway Programme (SP-SUTD APP) to get a head start in university life.
