DIPLOMA IN ELECTRICAL & ELECTRONIC ENGINEERING (S99, DEEE)

The Power to Fuel the Future

Dive into a diverse range of engineering subjects with the Diploma in Electrical & Electronic Engineering (DEEE) and develop versatile, industry-ready skillsets. With a prestigious history of over 65years and over 20,000 successful graduates, we have a track record of producing successful engineers that are highly sought-after in the field.

Read moreRead less

Advance industries and services with smart and sustainable technologies

Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that you have the diverse skillsets needed to tackle real-world engineering projects that make a tangible impact on society. You’ll master the skills to contribute to Singapore’s booming technological industry, such as AI, digitalisation, AR/VR and Internet of Things (IoT), while staying updated on sustainability principles. With these cutting-edge technologies and skillsets, you’ll be at the forefront of innovation, riding the wave of global growth.

Course Highlights

  • A broad-based curriculum with a choice from SEVEN specialisations in the third year: Biomedical, Communication, Microelectronics, Power, Rapid Transit, Robotics & Control, and Sustainable Energy (NEW) .

  • A curriculum that follows the  CDIO (Conceive-Design-Implement-Operate) framework, which is used in top universities in the United States, Europe and Australia.
  • Accelerated Pathway Programme: An opportunity to join the SP-NUS Accelerated Pathway Programme (SP-NUS APP) or SP-SUTD Accelerated Pathway Programme (SP-SUTD APP) to get a head start in university life.

What You Can Expect

  • Pursue your passion through electives that can lead to a Minor or Elective Certificate, such as Minor in 5G & Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT), and Certificate in Advanced Mathematics.
  • Enjoy direct 2nd-year entry and advanced standing to renowned local and overseas universities. Find out more about your further study options here.
Read moreRead less
  • Opportunities to join the premier EEE talent development programmes programmes and take part in local and overseas competitions.
  • Recognition by Energy Market Authority (EMA) of Singapore for the application of Electrical Technician License if specialised in Power.
  • Experience an augmented learning environment in rail engineering with our latest integrated Rail System Simulator, a first among the polytechnics. 
  • An edge in learning 5G wireless technology, in the first-of-its-kind 5G Garage, set up in collaboration with Singtel and Ericsson.
  • Gain exposure through Overseas Immersion Programmes in overseas countries, such as Japan, China, and Malaysia.
  • Immerse in a 22-week internship at reputable companies such as SP Group, SMRT, A*STAR, PSA, Siemens, ST Electronics and CleanTech Solar.

Other than practical skills, the course also trained us in the concepts of critical thinking and design thinking. With all the opportunities and help from my academic mentors, I was able to better plan my future endeavours.
