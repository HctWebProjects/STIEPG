Elevate your journey in Mechanical Engineering with our curriculum, seamlessly integrating AI advancements and sustainability principles. Beyond mastering the fundamentals, our curriculum providing a robust foundation in mechanical engineering coupled with essential skills in Business and Humanities. We believe in learning by doing, so we prioritise hands-on experiences and industry-focused opportunities that allow you to develop your knowledge, competencies and your character.

 

Choose from any of the six different specialisations in your second year: 

  1. Automation & Robotics 

  2. Biomedical 

  3. Energy & Facilities Management 

  4. Engineering Design & Simulation 

  5. Precision Engineering 

  6. Rapid Transit Technology

AI and DME

Have you ever wondered how Artificial Intelligence (AI) can play a part in saving lives? With technological advances, AI is now a ubiquitous presence in our daily routines.

As part of their Final Year Project (FYP), three students from the Diploma in Mechanical Engineering (DME) aspire to integrate AI into the medical field, offering valuable support to doctors and surgeons in making critical decisions and optimising surgical procedures. Currently situated in Professor Theodoros Kofidis’ lab at the Department of Surgery, Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine, National University of Singapore (NUS) and under the guidance of Research Assistant Kim Ki Han, this trio has gained hands-on experience with medical devices, enriching their understanding of cardiology throughout their FYP.
