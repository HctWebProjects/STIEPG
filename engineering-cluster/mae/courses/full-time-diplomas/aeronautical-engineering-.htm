Take your passion to new heights where Sky is NOT the limit!

Discover a thrilling runway to your dreams with our Diploma in Aeronautical Engineering (DARE) programme! Since its launch in 2002, SP has remained a trailblazer in aeronautical engineering education, making the DARE course one of Singapore’s most sought-after engineering diplomas.

Our curriculum is designed to meet industry standards set by the Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore (CAAS), ensuring you’ll graduate with the most up-to-date skills. You’ll receive hands-on training by ST Engineering Aerospace, our official training partner, with a focus on aircraft-related modules. Through a design thinking approach, you will foster problem-solving and creativity through hands-on learning. What’s more, you can personalise your learning journey with various CAAS-approved electives or extra courses. 

If you’re ready to mount new heights, the Diploma in Aeronautical Engineering is your gateway to an eye-opening adventure in the aerospace industry.

Why Choose DARE (S88)

DIVERSE ELECTIVE TRACKS FOR VERSATILITY IN AEROSPACE & AVIATION

Diverse Elective Tracks for versatility in Aerospace & Aviation

The 1st Aerospace related diploma in Singapore that offers 4 elective tracks option giving our students great versatility and wide variety of career options in the Aviation & Aerospace Industry.

  • Aircraft Maintenance and Composite Repair
  • Aerospace Design and Manufacturing
  • Aviation Management
  • Aerospace Emerging Technology

FUTURE READY BASED ON SINGAPORE INDUSTRY TRANSFORMATION MAPS

Future Ready based on Singapore Industry Transformation Maps

Space engineering + Artificial Intelligence (AI) + Internet of Things (IoT) + Cybersecurity + Robotics & Automation + Composites + Additive Manufacturing

STRONG INDUSTRY PARTNERSHIP + DIVERSITY IN CAREER CHOICES

 

Strong Industry Partnership + Diversity in Career Choices

Industry co-located facilities, Industry co-designed curriculum and industry practitioners to co-teach lessons.

ACCELERATED PATH FOR UNIVERSITY STUDIES + HIGH ACHIEVERS

Accelerated Path for University Studies

  • Our academic rigor allows our graduates to gain admission to local and overseas universities with module exemption and advanced standings based on merits, and complete related 4-year Engineering Degree with Honours in 3 years!
  • In 2022, 42 NTU Engineering Graduates with Highest Distinction and 1 Valedictorian were from SP DARE+DME+DMRO
  • In 2023, 15 NTU Engineering Graduates with Highest Distinction and 1 Valedictorian were from SP DARE+DME+DMRO

Course Highlights

State-of-the-art aircraft training facilities: 

The Aerohub equipped with four aircraft (Hawker 125-700A, King Air B90, A4SU Super Skyhawk and Bell UH-1H Helicopter), a dedicated Virtual Reality & Augment Reality Learning Space and full motion flight simulators to provide authentic aircraft related training experience.

Learn more

Relevant curriculum:

A curriculum that is aligned to the “Singapore Airworthiness Requirements Part 66” (SAR 66) specified by the Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore (CAAS)

Learn more

Electives mapped to the Aerospace Engineering and Air Transport skills framework:

  • Advanced Aerospace Design and Manufacturing
  • Advanced Aircraft Maintenance Practices and Aerospace Composite Repair
  • Fleet Technical Management
  • Aviation Management
  • Aerospace Emerging Technology

Learn more

Many other opportunities:

  • Opportunity to pursue a Private Pilot License (PPL) at the Singapore Youth Flying Club (SYFC).
  • Opportunity to participate in local and overseas competitions such as the Singapore Amazing Flying Machine Competition (SAFMC) and World Skills Competition (WSC).
  • Overseas exchange programme

INDUSTRY PROJECTS

The most comprehensive & versatile Aerospace course in Singapore!

What You Can Expect

  • Pursue a Private Pilot License (PPL) at the Singapore Youth Flying Club (SYFC).
  • Participate in local and overseas competitions such as the Singapore Amazing Flying Machine Competition (SAFMC) and World Skills Competition (WSC).
  • Explore different cultures during the overseas exchange programme.
Read moreRead less
  • Enjoy advanced standing in local and international universities.
  • The DARE curriculum prepares you for the CAAS Airworthiness Requirements (SAR 66) exams, enabling you to become a licensed Aircraft Maintenance Engineer ahead of others.
  • Look forward to attractive career opportunities in the rapidly expanding aerospace industry.

 

*Applicants should not be suffering from severe vision deficiency (including colour vision), acute hearing impairment or uncontrolled epilepsy. Interested applicants with these conditions are advised to contact us for more information.
