Integrating People, Place and Technology.

In today's digital era and emphasis on sustainability, buildings are envisioned to be smarter and greener. The Diploma in Facilities Management (DFM) will equip you with the skills to manage different types of building assets, from managing operations, to maintenance and integrating new technologies.

Explore IoT, sensors, building information modelling, digital asset delivery, data analytics and visualisation and other relevant technologies supporting the operations, maintenance, and management of facilities. Learn about environmental and sustainability concepts, certification schemes and asset enhancement strategies that contribute to sustainability and operational efficiency. 

Discover how to optimise energy usage, monitor building performance, and enhance user comfort through innovative and sustainable solutions to shape a better environment for all.

Facilities management is a critical discipline that plays a pivotal role in the efficient and sustainable operation of the built environment.  Through engaging industry-linked projects and internships, you'll gain invaluable hands-on experience, put theory into practice, and develop the practical skills. Plus, you'll earn certificates in Fire Safety Management, bizSAFE Level 2 (Risk Management), and Supervise Construction Work for WSH, giving you a competitive advantage when you join the industry!

Diploma in Facilities Management allows our graduates to receive generous advanced standing from reputable foreign universities and module exemptions from local universities.

For students who want to further accelerate their university journey, they can enrol into modules offered by the National University of Singapore, Bachelor of Engineering (Infrastructure & Project Management) as elective modules in SP, if they meet the relevant criteria. This means, students have the opportunity to experience first-hand, the university modules, culture and environment, while still being a student with DFM. The grades earned in these modules will be recognised by the NUS, if upon graduation choose to continue their undergraduate studies in NUS.

A world of exciting facilities management career opportunities awaits you, be it with property developers, service providers, government agencies and statutory boards. 

If you like working with people, managing different types of properties such as integrated resorts, hotels, shopping malls, airports, museums, stadiums, residences, schools, amusement parks, join the Diploma in Facilities Management course.

What You Can Expect

  • Participate in immersive out-of-classroom projects and gain practical knowledge of the industry through site visits.
  • Learn how to use data dashboards for monitoring, measuring, and analysing data such as indoor air quality and energy consumption to track trends, identify patterns, and communicate insights.
  • Enhance your professional profile with certificates in Fire Safety Management, bizSAFE Level 2 (Risk Management), and Supervise Construction Work for WSH.
  • Develop professional skills in a 22-week Internship Programme, at reputable organisations.
  • Participate in out-of-classroom projects and study trips, and gain practical knowledge of the industry through site visits to exciting places like Google Singapore, Siloso Beach Resort, Singapore Sports Hub and the Singapore Discovery Centre.
  • Gain entry and enjoy advanced standing at established universities.
  • A world of opportunities awaits you in the Built Environment sector. Find out about the career options and pathways available to DFM graduates here.
  • SP-NUS Accelerated Pathway Programme: Take up Modules taught by National University of Singapore and complete your degree earlier
