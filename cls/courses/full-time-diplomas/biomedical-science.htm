Diploma in Biomedical Science (DBS - S98)

The Science that ‘Saves Lives’

The science of saving lives takes centre stage here, where we focus on the life sciences and bio-pharmaceutical industries. The Diploma in Biomedical Science (DBS) offers you the opportunity to make a positive impact on the health and well-being of our community.

Read moreRead less

Through our comprehensive curriculum, students can choose from three exciting specialisations:

  • Medical Technology: Gain the skills to diagnose and manage human diseases by providing accurate and timely diagnosis with the use of medical technology.
  • Cardiac Technology: Save human lives, test heart functions to diagnose and intervene in heart related diseases.
  • Biotechnology: Harness the power of biological processes to improve lives and contribute to advancements and innovations in medical research and drug development.

Are you driven to make a lasting impact on the lives of those around you? Join us at the Diploma in Biomedical Science course and let your passion for discovery and dedication to humanity shine.

Course Highlights

  • Recognised by the American Society for Clinical Pathology (ASCP), USA
  • We are the only local diploma programme that provides training in Cardiac Technology in partnership with the National Heart Centre Singapore (NHCS)
  • Our graduates have achieved exceptional outcomes, with 45 successfully gaining admission into Medicine and 5 into Dentistry, showcasing the remarkable achievements of our program
  • Eighty of our graduates were awarded MOHH scholarships/awards and gained admission into competitive allied healthcare undergraduate programmes

What You Can Expect

  • Get a head start with opportunities to intern at multinational biopharmaceutical companies, renowned laboratories including A*STAR institutes and top-ranking overseas universities
  • Gain authentic learning experiences by training with the National Heart Centre Singapore for Cardiac Technology
Read moreRead less
  • Follow your passion. Take your pick from elective modules in Forensic Biology, Cytogenetics or Blood Banking to expand your interests
  • Enjoy direct entry and advanced standing to renowned local and overseas universities. Find out more about your further study options here
  • Explore a wide array of career options and pathways here

Medical Technology Suite

The Medical Technology Suite share purpose-built common facilities for practical and research work in the areas of clinical chemistry, haematology, histology, medical microbiology, immunology and molecular diagnostics.
